About Us

Is'baya Sethu is a proudly rural-based livestock enterprise rooted in Inanda, Durban. We deliver livestock with professionalism, empathy, and cultural sensitivity, understanding the profound significance of each animal for funerals, weddings, ancestral rites, and other traditional ceremonies. Our commitment is to provide healthy, culturally appropriate livestock with seamless logistics, honoring traditions with every delivery.

Our Services

Live Animal Delivery

Direct delivery of healthy, culturally appropriate cows and goats. Ideal for funerals, weddings, ancestral rites, and other significant events. We handle full logistics, ensuring the animals arrive safely and respectfully at your desired location.

Processed Cow Delivery

We offer professional slaughter, hygienic packaging, and delivery of high-quality processed meat (approximately 120–150kg meat per cow). Our team handles all cold-chain logistics, ensuring the meat arrives fresh and ready for your traditional feasts.
